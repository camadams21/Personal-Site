About Me

As an Actuarial Mathematics & Finance student at Bryant University, I am excited to apply my knowledge and skills to the financial services and insurance industries. Ever since I began collecting sports cards growing up, I have had a passion for statistics, and this passion has continued to grow as I discover new applications for statistical knowledge each day. One of my favorite units to examine are dollars, as I find it fascinating to dissect the underlying factors that yield an ultimate dollar amount. I have received my FINRA SIE Exam certification, and I am planning to take my first SOA actuarial exam this school year. I will be graduating in May 2025, and I am currently seeking entry-level opportunities for the summer of 2025.
